My research concerns identifying causal explanations from observational (non-experimental) data. Typical problems that I have worked on so far include
  • inferring the causal direction between a pair of random variables \((X, Y)\), and
  • finding the conditions under which a set of variables \((\mathcal{X})\) affect a target \((Y)\).

I am looking for new opportunities.
